Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

What we collect

Cue collects only what's necessary to run the app: your email address (for your account), and the reminders you create (names, dates, categories, notes). We do not collect your location, contacts, or any other personal data.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the Cue service — syncing your reminders across devices and sending you notifications. We do not sell your data to anyone, ever. We do not use your data for advertising.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. Each user can only access their own data — no other user can see your reminders. Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

Payments

Payments are handled entirely by Stripe. Cue never sees or stores your credit card information. Stripe is PCI-DSS compliant. You can review Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

Notifications

If you enable push notifications, your device's push subscription token is stored to send you reminders. You can turn off notifications at any time from within the app or your device settings.

Deleting your data

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us. We will remove all your data within 7 days.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at cue.helpcontact@gmail.com
